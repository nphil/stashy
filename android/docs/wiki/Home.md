## What is StashFlow?

StashFlow is an open-source Flutter companion app for browsing and managing your Stash media library.  
It runs on **Android**, **Windows**, **macOS**, **Linux**, and the **Web**.

### Key capabilities

- 📱 Adaptive UI — mobile-first on phones, navigation rail + wide grids on desktop
- 🎬 Native-feel video player with autoplay queue, subtitle support, and PiP (Android)
- 🖼️ Fullscreen image viewer with slideshow, zoom, and inline rating
- 🔎 Advanced filtering and sorting across Scenes, Images, Performers, Studios, Tags, Galleries, and Groups
- 🛠️ Metadata editor with scraper integration
- 🎲 Discovery tools: Random actions, Surprise Me, and Shake-to-Discover (Android)
